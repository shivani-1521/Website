Services

Making warehouses smarter and food grains safer.

Monitoring

Amber monitors food grains stored in a warehouse, round the clock, through wireless sensors. Our devices measure physical parameters to track grain health.

Pest Control

We detect pest growth at early stages through continous monitoring therby limiting the scale and scope of fumigation. We seek to help our partners reduce pesticide residue within the Maximum Residue Limit.

Analytics

Amber helps warehouses in planning liquidation of stocks, powered by data-driven analytics. We help in deciding when and which stock to liquidate.

About

A brief history of Amber.

  • 2016-17

    Beginnings

    Amber began as an academic project in late 2016 with aim of reducing loss of food grains in Indian Warehouses. It got it's name from the word अंबर (sky).

  • September 2017

    First Step

    Our prototype has been granted pre-incubation by Department of Science & Technology under the Nidhi-Prayas Scheme at icreate, Ahmedabad.

  • December 2017

    Progress

    We developed our second prototype, which is smaller and requires less power.

  • January 2018

    Twin Success

    We were runner-up at Indo-Israel Innovation Challenge. Additionaly we secured permission for field testing our prototypes from FCI, Jaipur.
